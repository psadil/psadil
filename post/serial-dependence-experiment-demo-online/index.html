serial dependence experiment demo, online

Last updated on May 31, 2021 1 min read dissertation

A breadcrumb trail of post topics suggests that I have been working on an experiment to explore serial dependence. When I explain these kinds of experiments to people outside of cognitive psychology, I find it hard to explain the experiment’s simplicity. The tasks are basic.

Today, I am happy to report that I now have an online demonstration of the task! The demo can be reached here. The experiment will start with a few pages of (poorly formatted) instructions. After that, you’ll do 11 trials.
